FreshFridge's Solution

FreshFridge's food spoilage prevention compartment uses multiple different types of gas sensors to detect what types of food have gone bad and warns the user about it by alerting our website.

PCB Schematic

FreshFridge gas sensor PCB schematic

Gas sensors operate on 5V input/output and needs a 4.7k resistive load. Used built-in breakout board to step up 3.3V to 5V to power the entire PCB. PCB used resistor dividers to convert non-I2C voltage output
