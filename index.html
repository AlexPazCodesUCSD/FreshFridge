FreshFridge's mission is to help prevent food spoilage in young adult fridges.

FreshFridge's Solution

FreshFridge is planning on creating a container that uses multiple different types of gas sensors to detect what types of food have gone bad and warns the user about it by sending notifications on an app. The device is primarily intended to be used within refrigerators and will provide both live physical detection and remote reminders.
